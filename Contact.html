Frequently Asked Questions.

How long does a bespoke consultation take?

A typical consultation lasts 90 minutes, during which we discuss your vision, take measurements, and explore design options.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring inspiration photos, details about your event, and any specific requirements. If you have a particular style in mind, visual references are very helpful.

How far in advance should I book my consultation?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for custom pieces, though we can accommodate rush orders when possible.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options for all our services. Details will be discussed during your consultation.
