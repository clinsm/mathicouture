Who we are.

Mathi's is an evening dress boutique in Kinshasa, Congo, born from the passion of its founder, a lifelong fashion enthusiast inspired by Ornelys Couture, a family business. Dedicated to beauty and elegance, Mathi's creates unique, artisan-quality dresses that celebrate femininity and style, ensuring every woman shines at her most memorable moments. 

Discover more about our story and creations at Mathi's – visit our About page to explore the world where every dress tells a story!
