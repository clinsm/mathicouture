About Us

Three generations of women united by passion, skill, and an unwavering commitment to creating extraordinary moments through exceptional couture.

Our Story.

The story of Mathi's begins in 1999 when Ornelys Mathis opened a small atelier in Kinshasa with nothing but a sewing machine, extraordinary talent, and an unwavering vision to create beautiful gowns for the women of Congo.

What started as Ornelys Couture became a family legacy, with each generation adding their unique perspective while preserving the essence of exceptional craftsmanship. Today, Mathi's represents the evolution of this heritage—a modern interpretation of traditional excellence.

We believe that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary, and every special moment deserves a gown that captures its significance. This philosophy guides everything we do, from our personalized consultations to our hand-finished details.
